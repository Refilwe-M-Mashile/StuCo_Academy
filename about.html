About Student Academy (StuCo)

Who we are?

Tutoring

Tutors can register and offer their services to registered students at TUT, for different modules of their choice.

Collaborating

Students can connect and chat with fellow students, and collaborate on common academic tasks.


Student Academy (StuCo) is an online platform made by students for students to enhance learning and academic performance. The platform is easy to access and is integrated with WhatsApp, an App that students use daily. Student Academy aims to provide help to students at the comfort of their homes. StuCo enables collaboration amongst students in a digital platform, thus making it easy to connect with anyone ou share classes with.
